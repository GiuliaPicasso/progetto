A WOLF IS A WOLF (1931)

Plot

In the animal village, a raccoon and a goat venture to eat persimmons but are attacked by a wolf. The raccoon escapes, but the goat is captured. Determined to defeat the wolf, the raccoon boasts of transforming into a big octopus. However, his attempts fail, and winter arrives. The village animals devise a plan involving a monkey, a rabbit, and a makeshift bridge to outsmart the wolf. As the wolf crosses the river, the bridge collapses, and the animals rejoice in their victory.
