THE GOLDEN FLOWER (1929)

Plot

During a village festival, Kagura performer Dangobei entertains with comical masks. After the festival, he gets lost and encounters a giant snake in the dark Kuragari-toge Pass. To save himself, Dangobei wears various masks, convincing the snake he's a raccoon dog. The snake spares him, but Dangobei's dislike for money unintentionally causes the snake's demise. When villagers hear the tale, they confront and kill the snake, who showers Dangobei with oval gold coins in its dying moments.
