Info

codex folklore is the collection of information derived from the research for a Master's thesis in Communication Design, focused on the translation and reinvention of Japanese folklore in animation and how anime manage to convey cultural meanings to a new audience. This database does not aim to trivialize a culture that comprises many more elements; it serves as a reference and reflection point to be expanded upon. The visible information results from a selection based on the exploration and identification of the folkloric significance in the portrayal of nature and the supernatural in anime. Further references for the research can be found at:
Japanese Animated Film Classics , Anilist.co , Yokai.com, Wikipedia.
