Info

Codice Folklore is the digital archive of the data collected during the research for the Master's thesis in Communication design, focused on the translation and reinvention of Japanese folklore in animation and how anime manage to convey cultural meanings to a new audience. Natural and supernatural elements from Japanese folklore were only taken into consideration, identifying and pinpointing them in the selected anime. References for the research can be found at the following online databases: Japanese Animated Film Classics , Anilist.co , Yokai.com, Wikipedia.

This research focuses on a portion of the whole symbolism behind the visual representation of folklore in Japanese traditional animation. Anyone who is interested in developing further analysis can freely take on from this research.
