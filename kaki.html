KAKI

Fruit of the gods

The Japanese persimmon, or kaki: scientific name, Diospyros kaki. Diospyros is often translated as “fruit of the Gods”; this may come as a surprise to most Japanese people as the humble kaki is a common fruit tree that has been planted in household gardens from long ago.
