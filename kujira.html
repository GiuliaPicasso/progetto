KUJIRA (1927)

Plot

The first one is the reworking done by Ofuji on one of his previous works and tells the cruel, fantastical, and surreal story of three shipwreck survivors who vie for the only surviving woman, get swallowed and spat out by a whale, and meet a tragic end. Nature and the mythical Japanese animal seem to seek justice for human cruelty.
