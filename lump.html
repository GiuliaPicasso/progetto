THE LUMP (1929)

Plot

In a tale of two old men with lumps, honest Tarobei and greedy Jirobei each have a lump on one cheek. Tarobei, caught in a storm, encounters long-nosed goblins during a moonlit party where his comical dance earns their favor. The great goblin takes Tarobei's "treasure" (his lump), making him happy. Jirobei, seeking reward, convinces Tarobei to let him attend the next party. However, Jirobei's self-centered dance angers the goblins, and in retaliation, they throw Tarobei's lump at him. Now, with lumps on both cheeks, Jirobei is blown away by the great goblin's fan.
