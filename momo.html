A LETTER TO MOMO (2011)

Plot

Following the premature loss of her father, Momo has to move with her mother to the old family house on a remote island. Her heart is still feeling uneasy about an unfinished letter left by her father. A letter that contained only two words: "Dear Momo..." What was dad going to say? One day, exploring the attic of her new big house, Momo finds a dusty and worn out book. And from that moment, something really unexpected starts happening around her...
