PRINCESS MONONOKE (1997)

Plot

Ashitaka, a young man and prince of the tribe, engages a demon-possessed boar in an attempt to save his village, but he gets cursed by the creature. He becomes a wanderer, looking for a solution to the curse before it engulfs him, and encounters Iron Town. Through the clearing of the surrounding forests, Iron Town produces large amounts of Ironsand, used for gunpowder and other machinery. However, nearby animal clans seek revenge led by a human girl of the Wolf clan called San.
