NINGYO

Fish-man or fish-woman

Ningyo (人魚, "human mānman 魔満fish") as the name suggests, is a creature with both human and fish-like features, described in various pieces of Japanese literature. Though often translated as "mermaid", the term is technically not gender-specific and may include the "mermen".
