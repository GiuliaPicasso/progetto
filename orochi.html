OROCHI, THE EIGHT-HEADED DRAGON (1963)

Plot

This anime film follows the journey of Susanoo, the god (depicted as a cute boy), who sets out to find his mother Izanami in the heavens after her passing. Accompanied by Akahana, a talking rabbit, and Taitanbo, a strong giant from the Land of Fire, Susanoo faces various challenges. In the Izumo Province, he befriends Princess Kushinada, and upon learning about the sacrifice of her seven sisters to the eight-headed serpent Orochi, Susanoo decides to help her family. Together with his companions, they prepare for a spectacular showdown to protect Kushinada and defeat Orochi once and for all.
