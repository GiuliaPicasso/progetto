SAKURA

Cherry blossom

The cherry blossom is considered the national flower of Japan, and is central to the custom of hanami (flower viewing). In Japan, cherry blossoms symbolize clouds due to their characteristic of blooming en masse, and are considered an enduring metaphor for the ephemeral nature of life. This traditional symbolism is associated with the influence of Shinto, embodied in the concept of mono no aware.
