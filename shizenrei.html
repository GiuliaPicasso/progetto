Shizenrei

自然霊

Since kami is the opposite of yōkai, anything that stays in the middle can be considered shizenrei. They are supernatural spirits, referring to natural phenomenon or animals regarded as divine messengers.

Explore each shizenrei by clicking on its respective folder, containing information about its representation in anime.
