SHIZENREI 自然霊

Since kami and yōkai are at the opposite poles of the same continuum, anything that stays in the middle can be considered shizenrei. They are supernatural nature spirits, referring to common natural phenomenon such as sunsets, or to animals considered divine messengers, sometimes even bordering on being regarded as deities.
