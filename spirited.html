SPIRITED AWAY (2001)

Plot

On the way to their new home, 10-year-old Chihiro Ogino's family stumbles upon a deserted theme park, inhabited by spirits. When Chihiro's mother and father eat food from a restaurant in the street, angry spirits turn them into pigs, leaving Chihiro trapped between the spirit world. With only her courage and some new found friends to aid her, Chihiro embarks on a journey to turn her parents back to their original forms and return home.
