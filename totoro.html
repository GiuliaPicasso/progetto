MY NEIGHBOR TOTORO (1988)

Plot

Satsuki and her four-year-old sister Mei have just moves into a new home in the countryside. To their delight they discover that their new neighbor is a mysterious forest spirit called Totoro, who introduces them to extraordinary characters, including a cat that doubles as a bus, takes them on a journey through the wonders of nature.
