TSUKUYOMI

God of the moon

Tsukuyomi-no-Mikoto (ツクヨミノミコト, 月読命), or simply Tsukuyomi (ツクヨミ, 月読), is the moon god in Japanese mythology and the Shinto religion. Tsukuyomi was the second of the "three noble children" born from Izanagi. There is so little known about Tsukuyomi that even their sex is unknown.
